The Best Movie According to Aong Sing

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather (1972)

I love The Godfather movie for its unforgettable storytelling, powerful performances, and deep exploration of family, loyalty, and power.
With iconic roles by Marlon Brando and Al Pacino, it delivers timeless drama that still feels intense and relevant today.

Interstellar (2014)

I love Interstellar Movie for its powerful mix of science and emotion—it's not just about space travel, but about love, sacrifice, and time.
With breathtaking visuals, a moving score by Hans Zimmer, and a deeply emotional father-daughter story, it stays with you long after it ends.

The Bourne Identity (2002)

I love The Bourne Identity movie for its intense action, smart storytelling, and realistic spy thriller vibe that keeps you hooked from start to finish.
With a gripping performance by Matt Damon, it delivers fast-paced chases, mystery, and a hero who relies on intelligence as much as skill.
